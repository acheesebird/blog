About the course

This course is part of the Analysics course and is a reference course for the entrance examination of the Graduate School of Mathematical Informatics at the University of Tokyo .

This course is a general introduction to Mathematical Analysis I, with selected materials that are profound and advanced, covering topics such as Real Analysis, Measure Theory, and Topology.
